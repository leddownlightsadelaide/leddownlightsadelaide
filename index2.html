Instantly Cut Your Lighting Costs

Compared to 12 volt halogen downlights, LED downlights typically use up to 75% less electricity for the same brightness. It is estimated that a household which changes all its lighting to efficient LEDs can save up to 5% on their total annual household electricity bill.

Additionally, LED downlights will last on average 50,000 hours, compared to just 2000 for halogens. To put this in perspective, if used for 8 hours a day every day, you can expect your LEDs to last for over 17 years.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are LED downlights dimmable?
A: Yes, LED downlights are dimmable. If you have existing dimming circuitry, this can used. If you require new dimmers, we are able to install these.

Q: Do LED downlights come in a range of colour temperatures (warm light / cool light)?
A: Yes, our downlights come in a range of colour temperatures, just let us know your preference. If you're undecided, we can demonstrate the difference in your home or even supply color temperature switchable downlights.

Q: What colour fittings do you offer?
A: Our downlights come in both ceiling white and brushed metal fittings.

Q: How long will an installation take?

Q: Do you charge a call out fee?
A: We do not charge call out fees. However, we do a have a minimum install/conversion quantity of five downlights.
